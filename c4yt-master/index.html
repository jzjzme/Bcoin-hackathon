ABOUT OUR EVENT

Crypto 4 Your Thoughts (C4YT) is a hackathon focused on fostering a strong and diverse open source community working to increase the accessibility of tools within the cryptocurrency ecosystem. C4YT will bring together 300 top engineers in Cryptocurrency, JavaScript/NodeJs communities, high-profile sponsors, speakers, and recruiters.

We will kick C4YT with an Opening Ceremony on the Friday night, and you’ll be able to start hacking immediately! The hackathon venue (Impact Hub San Francisco) will be open through Sunday evening when we conclude the event with a Closing Reception so you’re welcome to stay and hack any time. Breakfast, lunch, dinner and other refreshments will be provided. You can also expect morning yoga (optional), tech talks and workshops throughout the day and opportunities to network with the hiring companies and sponsors.
